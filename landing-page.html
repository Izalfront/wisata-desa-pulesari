Introduction Pulesari Village

Explore Pulesari tourism here

Pulesari Village is located in Turi District,
Sleman Regency, Yogyakarta Special Region,
Indonesia. This village is known for its
natural beauty and uniqueness as a tourist
destination that combines local culture
and natural charm.

White water rafting at Pulesari Tourism
Village offers an exciting adventure
along the river with beautiful natural
views. Suitable for all ages, this activity
combines challenge and natural beauty
in one unforgettable experience.
